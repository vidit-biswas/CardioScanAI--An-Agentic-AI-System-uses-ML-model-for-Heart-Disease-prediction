Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

Hypertension is a condition in which blood pressure remains consistently elevated over time.

Blood Pressure Categories:

Normal:

* Systolic below 120
* Diastolic below 80

Elevated:

* Systolic 120-129
* Diastolic below 80

Stage 1 Hypertension:

* Systolic 130-139
* Diastolic 80-89

Stage 2 Hypertension:

* Systolic 140 or higher
* Diastolic 90 or higher

Risk Factors:

* High sodium intake
* Obesity
* Lack of physical activity
* Smoking
* Excessive alcohol use
* Family history

Complications:

* Heart attack
* Stroke
* Kidney disease
* Heart failure
* Vision problems

Management:

* Reduce salt intake
* Exercise regularly
* Maintain a healthy weight
* Monitor blood pressure
* Follow prescribed treatment plans
