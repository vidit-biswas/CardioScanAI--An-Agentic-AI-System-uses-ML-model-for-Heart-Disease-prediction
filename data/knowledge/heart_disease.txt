Heart Disease Overview

Heart disease refers to a group of conditions that affect the heart and blood vessels. Coronary artery disease is the most common type and occurs when arteries become narrowed or blocked due to plaque buildup.

Common Risk Factors:

* High blood pressure
* High cholesterol
* Smoking
* Diabetes
* Obesity
* Physical inactivity
* Family history of heart disease
* Excessive alcohol consumption

Common Symptoms:

* Chest pain or discomfort
* Shortness of breath
* Fatigue
* Dizziness
* Palpitations
* Pain radiating to the arm, neck, jaw, or back

Prevention:

* Maintain a healthy weight
* Exercise regularly
* Eat a heart-healthy diet
* Avoid tobacco products
* Control blood pressure and cholesterol
* Manage stress effectively

Diagnosis:

* Electrocardiogram (ECG)
* Echocardiogram
* Stress test
* Blood tests
* Coronary angiography

Treatment:

* Lifestyle modifications
* Medications
* Angioplasty
* Stent placement
* Coronary artery bypass surgery
